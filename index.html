Community gardens can take a variety of forms including rooftop gardens, schoolyard gardens, backyard gardens and neighborhood gardens. Vegetables and other produce in community gardens are grown for the producers themselves or for other people in the community and are usually nonprofit endeavors. In contrast, farms tend to have greater capacity to grow larger quantities of produce and producers may sell their produce in local outlets..
